About Skillsync

Transforming how athletes and fitness enthusiasts connect with the perfect trainers

Our Mission

At Skillsync, we believe everyone deserves access to personalized training that matches their unique goals, learning style, and personality. Our mission is simple: to connect individuals seeking personal or professional growth with trainers who truly understand and align with their needs.

Founded in Oakland

Founded by Colin Wicks in Oakland, California, Skillsync was born from a simple observation: people often struggled to find trainers who not only have the right skills but also truly understand their learning style and personal preferences.

The Vision

With that vision, Skillsync was created as a platform focused on personalized trainer-client connections, making it easier than ever to find your perfect training match.
